Our Services

Event Planning

We handle your event from start to finish, ensuring every detail is carefully planned and executed. From concept development to final delivery, we turn your ideas into a memorable experience.

Styling and Coordination

Our team creates a beautiful and well-coordinated look for your event. We manage colors, themes, and overall aesthetics while coordinating activities on the event day to ensure everything runs smoothly.

Decorations and Layout

We provide creative and elegant decorations tailored to your event type. Our layout designs maximize space, enhance beauty, and create a welcoming atmosphere for guests.

Venue Sourcing & Invitations

We help you find the perfect venue that suits your event size, theme, and budget. We also assist with invitation planning to ensure your guests receive clear and timely event details.

Vendors Management

We work with trusted vendors and manage them on your behalf—caterers, photographers, entertainers, and more—so you don’t have to worry about coordination or delays.

Rentals (Chairs, Tables, Covers & Chafing Dishes)

We provide quality event rentals including chairs, tables, table covers, plates, cups cutlery and chafing dishes to give your event a complete and polished look.

After Event Cleanup

Once the event is over, we take care of the cleanup, leaving the venue neat and orderly so you can relax and enjoy the success of your event.
